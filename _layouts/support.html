Bull City Roller Derby Sponsors


As a fully volunteer league, we are extremely grateful to the sponsors who make our training, travel, home bouts (July 2019!), and community involvement possible. Thanks to each of our sponsors for their support of inclusive athletic leadership in Durham.

  • Founding Sponsor - Honeygirl Meadery, https://www.facebook.com/HoneygirlMeadery/, with the Honeygirl logo (attached here)

Sponsorship


Bull City Roller Derby Sponsorship Opportunities Explore BCRD sponsorship packages here. (link to attached PDF of 2019 packages) To discuss the right sponsorship package for your organization, email bullcityrollerderby@gmail.com. BCRD will host it's FIRST EVER Durham home bout in July 2019! Support this league accomplishment with an advertisement in the bout booklet, or contribute items to the bout day raffle. Raffle proceeds will be split between BCRD and a charitable partner. For information about bout day advertisements and raffle contributions, email bullcityrollerderby@gmail.com. What will your sponsorship of Bull City Roller Derby fund? Your gift will support inclusive athletic leadership in Durham. Your gift will enable us to welcome new skaters with bi-annual “booty camp” recruitment events and available gear. Your gift will help make uniforms accessible to all of our current and future teammates. Your gift will subsidize our away travel adventures. With extra expenses of transportation, lodging, and meals, we need your support to take our Durham spirit out into the world. Your gift will allow us to host public programs that promote inclusion and celebrate diversity in derby and the Durham community. Your gift will provide us the opportunity to expand our youth engagement efforts throughout the community, with a focus on athletic leadership. Your gift will help us start saving towards long-term league goals, including establishing a dedicated space for practice, scrimmages, and home bouts. In recognition of unique financial barriers that may impact women, POC, and/or LGBTQ+ business owners, BCRD is committed to discussing flexible sponsorship package rates that allow everyone to support the athletic community we are building.

Volunteer


Bull City Roller Derby Volunteers Thank you to all of our amazing volunteers, for your support of BCRD! If you love the idea of being part of what makes BCRD incredible, if you want to meet new folx, or if you want to support inclusive athletics in Durham, this is your thing! Want to be a BCRD volunteer? Email bullcityrollerderby@gmail.com to learn more. For our FIRST EVER home bout (July 2019), we need lots of volunteers to help things run smoothly, including: Non-Skating Officials (NSO) - we will train you! Medics to help keep our skaters safe. Front entrance, raffle, merch staff, and more! Don't see see your match? Email bullcityrollerderby@gmail.com with ideas about your skills and how they are a great fit for BCRD.
